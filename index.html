What Is a Hero Image?

A hero image is a large photo, illustration, video, or banner placed near the top of a webpage. It usually stretches across most or all of the screen and is designed to quickly catch the visitor’s attention.

Sometimes called a hero header or hero section, it is often the first major thing a visitor notices. A strong hero image can help communicate the purpose of the site and encourage people to keep exploring.

Why Do People Use Hero Images?

Web users are highly visual, so a large image near the top of a page can help create a strong first impression. The right image can quickly communicate the tone, topic, or purpose of a website.

Hero images can also support a headline, highlight a product or idea, and guide visitors toward an important action such as learning more, viewing a gallery, or contacting the site owner.
